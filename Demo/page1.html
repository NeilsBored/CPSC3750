I'm Shane...

Welcome to my little corner of the internet.
I’m someone who believes that movies and shows are more than just entertainment—they’re emotional time machines, mirrors of the human condition, and sometimes, just beautifully crafted nonsense.
What started as just watching whatever was on TV turned into late nights overanalyzing every shot in Blade Runner 2049 or debating whether Atlanta was a wild trip or a therapy session.
Here, I share why movies and shows mean so much to me. It’s not just the visuals, the writing, or the acting—though all of those matter. It’s the feeling I get when a story sticks with me long after the credits roll.
Whether it’s a childhood favorite or a mind-bender I just watched last week, visual media helps me understand myself—and maybe even the world—a little better.
